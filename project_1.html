Website for a local restaurant

First "big" test that me and my study group were prsented with - to build a responsive static webpage after the first semester of studies on Multimedia Design.

The focus was to have a clear Visual Identity and good Information Architecture to improve user experience and make the website intuitive for its visitors.

The restaurant provided us with the case where the help was needed to its B2C client side.

Before the making of the actual website many steps before were done:

  • - Thorough project planning
  • - User Research
  • - Collection of data
  • - Low and high fidelity mock-ups
  • - Structuring of content (Information Architecture)
  • - Basic SEO optimization

My roles in the project:
  • - User research (collection of interviews)
  • - UI Designer
  • - Frontend Developer
Technologies and tools used in the project:
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Adobe XD
  • Photoshop
  • Trello
